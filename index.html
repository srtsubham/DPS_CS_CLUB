About

About Us

Welcome to our Computer Club's official website of DPS Shaheedhpath.

Here we have special emphasis given on computer education .The school has a state of art computer lab. Computer science as a subject is compulsorily taken up for all students from nursery to X and beyond. The entire curriculum is well integrated with IT. Students also learn web designing, animation and other related and relevant computational skills. Some facilities in our club are following:

  • We have more than 100+ Computer setups ready-to-use for students. Also, we have school(club)-hours free internet access through high speed internet Wi-Fi.
  • We have high-level firewall security on our networking traffic to prevent unconditional threats coming around from the world through internet to maintain a secure environment.
  • Also we have a team of sophisticated teachers and organizers to guide students and maintain the whole club related issues, which is our first priority.

This website is specially for school regulated events and there results for ease of students. We are extending our student's limits in there ideas, because we believe "Computer is not a device anymore. It is an extension of your mind and your gateway to other people."
