Workshop

DPS Shaheedpath successfully organised Student Correspondent program in association with LIVE TODAY Media Group. A workshop was conducted by Live Today for the students of IX to XII to make them aware of the importance of media. The students who opted for the program will now be given training for becoming the future media reporters.
